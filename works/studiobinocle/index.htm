--- layout: works hash: studiobinocle project: studio binocle --- Our office is located atop of an unusual building from the 30’s known by people from Milan as ‘Cremlino’. The dome, under which we moved in 2014, measures 9 m in diameter, 5.5 m in height, overlooks the entire city and culminates into a 12 m spire.
